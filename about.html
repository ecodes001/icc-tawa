About Tawa Christian Centre Church

Who We Are

ICC-Tawa is a branch of the International Christian Centre, grounded in biblical teaching, vibrant worship, and community outreach. Our vision is to connect people to God and each other, challenge believers to grow in faith, and change the world through Christ’s love.

Our Mission

To make disciples of all nations by spreading the Gospel and serving communities in love and truth.

Our Core Values

  • Connecting people to God and each other
  • Challenging spiritual growth and discipleship
  • Changing communities through service and love
